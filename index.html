Walking Tours

Select a tour to see the famous landmarks featured on each one.

Each tour includes a complimentary tapas style lunch.

Barcelona would not be the city it is without the amazing work of Antonio Gaudi. Gaudi's unique, creative and un-mistakable work can be admired across the city.

This walking tour will start at one of Gaudi's most famous works, La Sagrada Familia, and will end with an exploration of Gaudi's fascinating Parc Guell. Passing along the way other wonders such as his Casa Batllo and Casa Mila.

Starting at the Basilica De Santa Maria del Pi, this tour will take you on a journey through Barcelona's Gothic Quarter, host to some of the city's most historic buildings.

Barcelona is a city rich in arts and music venues. During this tour, you will explore some of the city's oldest venues and how the city developed at the beginning of the 20th century.

Our urban tour explores how Barcelona transformed in the mordern era with the birth of of transport. The city is linked together with tram, underground and linked to the rest of the world with its trains stations and airport.

With the increasing concerns over climate change and the environment, cities worldwide are looking into sustainable architecture and environmentally friendly designs. On this tour, you will be guided around the city's most modern, sustainable building and reflect on the future of the city's skyline
