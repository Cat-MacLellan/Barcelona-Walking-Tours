Gaudi

Barcelona would not be the city it is without the amazing work of Antonio Gaudi. Gaudi's unique, creative and un-mistakable work can be admired across the city. This walking tour will start at one of Gaudi's most famous works, La Sagraga Familia, and will end with an exploration of Gaudi's fascinating La Parc Guell. Passing along the way other wonders such as his Casa Batllo and Casa Mila.
Sangrada
One of gaudi's most famous work, La Sagrada Familia has been in construction since 1892 and is not due to be finished until 2026 - a century after Gaudi's death. Gaudi combined Gothic with curvilinear Art Nouveu forms and his own geomentic style to create this masterpiece.
Parc Guell
Parc Guell was built between 1900 and 1914. This is a garden complex that has a series of dynamically designed buildings, including Gaudi's house.

Gothic Quarter

Starting at the La Basilica De Santa Maria del Pi, this tour will take you on a journey through Barcelona's Gothic Quarter, host to some of the city's most historic buildings, such as the famous La Cathedral Del Santa Eulaia
La Basilica De Santa Maria del Pi
This 15th century gothic church features a rose stained glass window on the front facade, above the gothic arch of the main entrance. The church also has a functioning bell tower. The largest of the six bells, weighing 1806kg, has been named Antonia.
